% Introduction
I explore the future of design. 
My research focuses on creating interaction systems and design tools with the goal of helping designers better understand people and create human-centered products.
I blend a background in mechanical engineering, mechatronics, computing, data collection, and product design to develop new interactive systems and to understand how designers do design. 
My work looks at observing people using intelligent systems and designing new kinds of interactive devices. 
My research has implications for human-robot interaction, autonomous cars, and human-centered artificial intelligence. 
My teaching aims to provide designers the skills to use new technologies and develop systems that focus on the needs of people.
